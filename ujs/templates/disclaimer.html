Data Source & Coverage

All court records are sourced from the Pennsylvania Unified Judicial System (UJS) portal, which is a public resource maintained by the Administrative Office of Pennsylvania Courts.

GavelSearch currently covers Lehigh County and Northampton County, including:

  • Court of Common Pleas (criminal, civil, family)
  • Magisterial District Judge courts
